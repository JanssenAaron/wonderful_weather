Cloud Formations

An important way to remain aware about the weather situation in which you may find yourself is cloud formations. Wispier clouds may indicate fair weather, while large intimidating cumulonimbus clouds may indicate an encroaching storm. Being aware of the clouds around you allows you to stay safe even without official warning systems.

Contrails

These clouds are often formed by aircraft, and in fair weather may be some of the only clouds in the sky.
